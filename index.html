About Marathon

The KGP Marathon began with a vision to inspire a healthier, more active lifestyle among students and the community. It’s not just about running — it's about pushing personal limits, fostering camaraderie, and celebrating the spirit of endurance. Each step taken represents a commitment to fitness, resilience, and mental strength, empowering participants to carry that energy beyond the finish line.
